Data Privacy Notice

This Prototype Hospital Information System by MSH collects patient information for healthcare documentation, continuity of care, patient monitoring, treatment planning, clinical communication, and institutional record keeping.

Data is collected through direct user input by authorized personnel, including but not limited to patient demographic details, assessments, FDAR notes, medication records, laboratory data, doctor’s orders, vital signs, intake and output records, and related clinical documentation.

The information entered in this system shall be used only for legitimate healthcare, academic, clinical simulation, and authorized patient-care-related purposes, in accordance with confidentiality obligations and applicable privacy standards.

Records shall be retained and disposed of in accordance with applicable retention requirements. For this prototype notice, patient records may be retained for up to 25 years, or released and transferred when there is a lawful transfer of care to another hospital or healthcare facility, subject to proper authorization and documentation.

By clicking I Agree, the user acknowledges responsibility to protect patient confidentiality and to use this system only within authorized and ethical healthcare practice.
