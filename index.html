Data Privacy Notice

This Prototype Hospital Information System by MSH is intended for authorized academic and clinical simulation use only. Patient information encoded in this system must be treated as confidential health information and must not be accessed, shared, copied, or disclosed without proper authorization.

The system collects only necessary information for documentation and monitoring, including patient demographics, assessments, vital signs, intake and output, doctor’s orders, IV therapy, medication records, laboratory requests, and FDAR nursing notes.

Data is collected through direct user input by an authorized user. The user is responsible for ensuring accuracy, privacy, confidentiality, and ethical use of the encoded electronic health record.

To safeguard patient information, access is controlled through login authentication, and users must review and agree to this notice before using the system. Users must log out after use, avoid leaving the device unattended, and handle printed patient charts securely.

Records may be retained for up to 25 years or transferred lawfully during transfer of care to another hospital or healthcare facility. After the retention period or once records are no longer needed, digital records shall be permanently deleted from browser/local storage, while printed records shall be shredded or securely disposed of to prevent unauthorized access or disclosure.

By clicking I Agree, the user acknowledges responsibility to protect patient confidentiality and to use this electronic health record ethically and only for legitimate healthcare, academic, or clinical simulation purposes.
